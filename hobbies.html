My hobbies

So that you can get to know me a little better, I'll provide you with a list of my hobbies and things I enjoy doing. Over the years, I've explored various activities, and these are my favorites.

  • Traveling: I absolutely love exploring new places and immersing myself in different cultures.
  • Drawing: Sketching is my way of capturing the beauty around me and expressing my creative ideas.
  • Studying Leanguages: I'm fascinated by the languages and cultures, and learning japanese, enaglish, france, etc is a fulfilling challenge.
  • Outdoor Exploration: Hiking and camping have become cherished hobbies, allowing me to connect with nature and appreciate its beauty up close.
  • Cultural Workshops: Attending workshops on traditional crafts during my travels, like pottery or weaving, lets me dive deeper into local cultures and their traditions.
  • Nature Sketching: Immersing myself in natural settings while sketching landscapes and wildlife helps me find tranquility and inspiration.

These hobbies not only complement my existing interests but also allow me to continue learning, growing, and connecting with others in unique ways.
